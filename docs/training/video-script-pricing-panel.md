# Pricing Panel Video Walkthrough Script

**Duration**: ~8 minutes
**Target Audience**: Pricing Analysts and Managers
**Last Updated**: 2025-12-14

---

## Video Structure

1. Introduction (0:00-0:30)
2. Dashboard Overview (0:30-1:30)
3. Competitor Prices (1:30-3:00)
4. Price History (3:00-4:00)
5. Price Comparison Matrix (4:00-5:00)
6. Margin Analysis (5:00-6:00)
7. Price Alerts (6:00-7:00)
8. Importing Data (7:00-7:30)
9. Conclusion (7:30-8:00)

---

## Script

### 1. Introduction (0:00-0:30)

**[SCREEN: Login page]**

> "Welcome to the Silvertree Pricing Panel. I'm [Name], and in this video, I'll show you how to track competitor prices, analyze margins, and set up automated price alerts."

**[ACTION: Log in as pricing-analyst@silvertreebrands.com]**

> "Let's log in to the Pricing panel."

---

### 2. Dashboard Overview (0:30-1:30)

**[SCREEN: Pricing Dashboard]**

> "This is the Pricing dashboard. At the top, you'll see key metrics."

**[ACTION: Point to each KPI]**

> "- **Products Tracked**: How many products have competitor price data
> - **Average Price Position**: Are you typically cheaper or more expensive than competitors?
> - **Recent Price Changes**: Count of competitor price updates this week
> - **Active Alerts**: Number of price alerts currently triggered"

**[ACTION: Scroll to price position chart]**

> "This chart shows your price positioning:
> - How many products you're cheapest on (green)
> - How many you're mid-range (yellow)
> - How many you're most expensive on (red)"

**[ACTION: Scroll to recent changes table]**

> "Below that, recent competitor price changes with the product, competitor, old price, new price, and percentage change."

---

### 3. Competitor Prices (1:30-3:00)

**[SCREEN: Competitor Prices page]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Competitor Prices' in navigation]**

> "The Competitor Prices page shows side-by-side pricing across all competitors."

**[ACTION: Point to table columns]**

> "For each product, you'll see:
> - Your current price
> - Competitor 1, 2, 3 prices (e.g., Takealot, Wellness Warehouse, Checkers)
> - Your price position
> - Last update timestamp"

**[ACTION: Scroll through table]**

> "Notice the color coding:
> - **Green**: You're the cheapest
> - **Yellow**: You're mid-range
> - **Red**: You're the most expensive"

**[ACTION: Use filters]**

> "Filter by category, price position, or competitor to focus on specific products."

**[ACTION: Click filter 'Most Expensive']**

> "Let's see products where we're most expensive..."

**[SCREEN: Filtered results]**

> "These are repricing opportunities. You can decide whether to lower your price, justify the premium, or discontinue the product."

**[ACTION: Sort by price difference]**

> "Sort by price difference to see where you're significantly more expensive than competitors."

---

### 4. Price History (3:00-4:00)

**[SCREEN: Price History page]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Price History' in navigation]**

> "Price History shows how competitor prices have changed over time."

**[ACTION: Select a product]**

> "Select a product from the dropdown..."

**[SCREEN: Price history chart]**

> "Here's the price trend chart. The solid line is your price, and dashed lines are competitors."

**[ACTION: Zoom to different time ranges]**

> "Zoom to 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or a custom range."

**[ACTION: Hover over data points]**

> "Hover over any point to see the exact price and date."

**[ACTION: Point out trends]**

> "Look for patterns:
> - Competitor price drops (opportunities to match)
> - Seasonal changes (holiday pricing)
> - Promotional periods (temporary dips)"

---

### 5. Price Comparison Matrix (4:00-5:00)

**[SCREEN: Price Comparison Matrix page]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Price Comparison Matrix' in navigation]**

> "The Price Comparison Matrix gives you a spreadsheet-style view of all prices at once."

**[ACTION: Scroll through matrix]**

> "Each row is a product, each column is a retailer. Green highlights the cheapest price in each row, red highlights the most expensive."

**[ACTION: Apply filters]**

> "Filter by category to focus on specific product groups."

**[ACTION: Toggle 'Show Only Where We're Most Expensive']**

> "Or toggle this filter to see only repricing opportunities."

**[ACTION: Sort by product name]**

> "Sort alphabetically or by any other column."

> "Use this view to quickly identify:
> - Products where you're overpriced
> - Products where you can raise prices
> - General market pricing trends"

---

### 6. Margin Analysis (5:00-6:00)

**[SCREEN: Margin Analysis page]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Margin Analysis' in navigation]**

> "Margin Analysis combines pricing with cost data to show profitability."

**[ACTION: Point to table columns]**

> "For each product:
> - Your cost
> - Your selling price
> - Gross margin percentage
> - Competitor average price
> - What your margin would be if you matched competitor average"

**[ACTION: Scroll through products]**

> "Color coding shows margin health:
> - **Green**: Margin > 30% (healthy)
> - **Yellow**: Margin 15-30% (moderate)
> - **Red**: Margin < 15% (low)"

**[ACTION: Click on a low-margin product]**

> "For low-margin products, you can:
> - Negotiate lower cost from supplier
> - Increase efficiency
> - Raise price slightly
> - Discontinue if unprofitable"

**[ACTION: Click on a high-margin + cheapest product]**

> "For high-margin products where you're also cheapest, you might be able to raise prices and still remain competitive."

> "The 'Margin if Matched' column helps you decide if matching competitor prices is worth it financially."

---

### 7. Price Alerts (6:00-7:00)

**[SCREEN: Price Alerts page]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Price Alerts' in navigation]**

> "Price Alerts notify you automatically when important price changes occur."

**[ACTION: Click 'New Alert']**

**[SCREEN: Create Alert form]**

> "To create an alert, click 'New Alert' and configure:
> - **Product**: Which product to track (or all products)
> - **Competitor**: Which competitor (or any)
> - **Alert Type**: Choose from four types"

**[ACTION: Show alert type dropdown]**

> "Alert types:
> 1. **Competitor Beats Our Price**: Alert when competitor goes below your price
> 2. **Price Below Threshold**: Alert when any price drops below X
> 3. **Price Change > X%**: Alert when price changes by more than X%
> 4. **Out of Stock**: Alert when competitor goes out of stock"

**[ACTION: Fill in form]**

> "Let's create an alert for when Takealot beats our price on Coconut Oil."

**[ACTION: Set threshold and activate]**

> "Set the threshold if needed, make sure it's active, and save."

**[ACTION: Navigate back to alerts list]**

> "Your alerts are listed here. You can edit, delete, or temporarily disable them."

**[ACTION: Click notification bell icon in header]**

> "When an alert triggers, you'll receive an email and an in-app notification here."

**[SCREEN: Notification dropdown]**

> "Click to see details, then mark as read."

---

### 8. Importing Data (7:00-7:30)

**[SCREEN: Import page or settings]**

> "Competitor prices must be imported via CSV or API. Let me show you the CSV format."

**[ACTION: Show sample CSV]**

> "Your CSV needs these columns:
> - product_sku
> - competitor_name
> - competitor_price
> - competitor_url (optional)
> - competitor_sku (optional)
> - in_stock (true/false)
> - scraped_at (timestamp)"

**[ACTION: Upload CSV]**

> "Upload your file, review the preview, and click Import."

**[SCREEN: Import results]**

> "You'll see results showing successful imports and any errors."

> "For automated imports, contact your administrator to set up API integration or scheduled CSV uploads."

---

### 9. Conclusion (7:30-8:00)

**[SCREEN: Dashboard]**

> "That's the Pricing Panel! To recap:
> - **Dashboard**: Monitor price positioning at a glance
> - **Competitor Prices**: Side-by-side price comparison
> - **Price History**: Track price changes over time
> - **Price Comparison Matrix**: Spreadsheet view of all prices
> - **Margin Analysis**: Combine pricing with profitability
> - **Price Alerts**: Automated notifications for price changes
> - **Import**: Bring in competitor price data"

> "For detailed instructions, see the Pricing Panel User Guide. If you have questions, contact support@silvertreebrands.com."

> "Thanks for watching!"

**[SCREEN: Fade out]**

## Post-Production Notes

### Visual Overlays

Add text overlays for:
- Alert types (clear explanation of each)
- CSV column names
- Color coding legend (green/yellow/red)

### Callouts

Use arrows or highlights for:
- Price position indicators
- Filter controls
- Export and import buttons
- Alert notification bell

### Demo Data

Use realistic competitor names:
- Takealot
- Wellness Warehouse
- Checkers
- Pick n Pay

Use realistic prices:
- R 50 - R 500 range for most products
- Show some price competitiveness, not all products most expensive

---

## Recording Tips

1. **Prepare CSV** with sample data for import demo
2. **Set up alerts** beforehand so you can show triggered alerts
3. **Use color-coded products** to clearly demonstrate green/yellow/red
4. **Zoom browser** to ensure all columns are visible
5. **Practice navigation** between pages smoothly
