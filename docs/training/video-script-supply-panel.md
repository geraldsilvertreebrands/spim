# Supply Panel Video Walkthrough Script

**Duration**: ~8 minutes
**Target Audience**: Suppliers (Basic and Premium tiers)
**Last Updated**: 2025-12-14

---

## Video Structure

1. Introduction (0:00-0:30)
2. Dashboard and KPIs (0:30-2:00)
3. Products Page (2:00-3:30)
4. Trends Analysis (3:30-4:30)
5. Premium Features Overview (4:30-6:30)
6. Exporting Data (6:30-7:30)
7. Conclusion (7:30-8:00)

---

## Script

### 1. Introduction (0:00-0:30)

**[SCREEN: Login page]**

> "Welcome to the Silvertree Supply Insights Portal. I'm [Name], and in this video, I'll show you how to track your brand's performance, analyze trends, and optimize your sales strategy."

**[ACTION: Log in as supplier-premium@test.com]**

> "Let's log in. Once authenticated, you'll land on the Supply dashboard."

---

### 2. Dashboard and KPIs (0:30-2:00)

**[SCREEN: Supply Dashboard]**

> "This is your Supply Insights dashboard. At the top, you'll see key performance indicators for your selected period."

**[ACTION: Point to each KPI tile]**

> "- **Total Sales**: Your revenue for the period
> - **Units Sold**: Total quantity sold
> - **Average Order Value**: Average basket size
> - **Unique Customers**: Number of buyers
> - **Gross Margin**: Your profit margin percentage
> - **Stock Coverage**: Days of inventory remaining"

**[ACTION: Hover over a KPI to show tooltip]**

> "Each KPI shows a percentage change versus the previous period. Green means growth, red means decline."

**[ACTION: Click period dropdown]**

> "You can change the date range here—last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, year to date, or a custom range."

**[ACTION: Select 'Last 90 Days']**

> "Let's look at the last 90 days... and the KPIs update immediately."

**[ACTION: Scroll to charts]**

> "Below the KPIs, you'll see charts showing:
> - Sales trend over time
> - Top-selling products
> - Category breakdown"

**[ACTION: If multiple brands, click brand dropdown]**

> "If you manage multiple brands, use this dropdown to switch between them."

---

### 3. Products Page (2:00-3:30)

**[SCREEN: Products page]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Products' in navigation]**

> "The Products page shows sales performance for each of your products."

**[ACTION: Point to table columns]**

> "Here you can see:
> - Product name and SKU
> - Units sold
> - Revenue
> - Average selling price
> - Current stock level
> - Stock coverage in days"

**[ACTION: Use search bar]**

> "Let me search for a specific product..."

**[ACTION: Type search query]**

> "Great! Here are the results."

**[ACTION: Click column header to sort]**

> "You can sort by any column. Let's sort by revenue to see top sellers."

**[ACTION: Use filters]**

> "Use filters to narrow down by category, stock status, or price range."

**[ACTION: Click on a product (if Premium)]**

> "Premium users can click a product for deep dive analytics—customer demographics, purchase patterns, and bundling opportunities."

---

### 4. Trends Analysis (3:30-4:30)

**[SCREEN: Trends page]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Trends' in navigation]**

> "The Trends page shows how your sales have changed over time."

**[ACTION: Select different metrics]**

> "You can view trends for:
> - Revenue
> - Units sold
> - Unique customers
> - Average order value"

**[ACTION: Change granularity]**

> "Adjust the time granularity—daily, weekly, or monthly."

**[ACTION: Select comparison mode]**

> "And compare to previous period or same period last year to identify seasonality."

**[ACTION: Hover over chart points]**

> "Hover over any point to see exact values and dates."

---

### 5. Premium Features Overview (4:30-6:30)

**[SCREEN: Premium features menu]**

> "Premium tier users have access to advanced analytics. Let me show you a few highlights."

**[ACTION: Click 'Forecasting']**

**[SCREEN: Forecasting page]**

> "**Forecasting** predicts future demand for your products. You'll see 30, 60, and 90-day forecasts with confidence intervals—best case, expected, and worst case."

**[ACTION: Scroll to recommended orders]**

> "The system even recommends reorder quantities based on current stock and forecast demand."

**[ACTION: Click 'Cohorts']**

**[SCREEN: Cohorts page]**

> "**Cohort Analysis** tracks customer retention. Each cohort represents customers who made their first purchase in a specific month."

**[ACTION: Point to retention grid]**

> "This grid shows what percentage returned in month 2, 3, 4, and so on. Use this to understand customer lifetime value."

**[ACTION: Click 'RFM Analysis']**

**[SCREEN: RFM page]**

> "**RFM Analysis** segments your customers by:
> - Recency: When did they last buy?
> - Frequency: How often do they buy?
> - Monetary: How much do they spend?"

**[ACTION: Scroll to segments]**

> "You'll see customer segments like:
> - Champions: Your best customers—frequent, recent, high spenders
> - At Risk: Used to buy often, now lapsed
> - Lost: Haven't bought in a long time"

**[ACTION: Click export on RFM page]**

> "Export these segments for targeted marketing campaigns."

**[ACTION: Click 'Retention']**

**[SCREEN: Retention page]**

> "**Retention** shows what percentage of customers return within 30, 60, or 90 days."

**[ACTION: Point to retention curve]**

> "The retention curve helps you understand when customers typically churn."

**[ACTION: Quickly show other premium pages]**

> "Other premium features include:
> - **Product Deep Dive**: Detailed analytics per product
> - **Supply Chain**: Inventory turnover and stock health
> - **Purchase Orders**: PO tracking and delivery rates
> - **Market Share**: Your position vs competitors
> - **Marketing**: Campaign performance and ROI"

---

### 6. Exporting Data (6:30-7:30)

**[SCREEN: Any page with export button]**

**[ACTION: Click 'Export' button]**

> "Every page has an export button. Click it to download data as CSV or Excel."

**[ACTION: Click 'CSV']**

> "Let's export as CSV..."

**[SCREEN: Download notification]**

> "The file downloads immediately. Open it in Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis."

**[ACTION: Open downloaded file in Excel]**

> "Here's the data—same as what you see on screen, ready for pivot tables, charts, or reporting."

---

### 7. Conclusion (7:30-8:00)

**[SCREEN: Dashboard]**

> "That's the Supply Insights Portal! To recap:
> - **Dashboard**: Monitor KPIs at a glance
> - **Products**: Track performance by product
> - **Trends**: Understand sales patterns over time
> - **Premium Features**: Forecasting, cohorts, RFM, and more
> - **Export**: Download data for offline analysis"

> "Upgrading to Premium unlocks forecasting, customer segmentation, and advanced analytics. Contact sales@silvertreebrands.com to upgrade."

> "For detailed instructions, see the Supply Panel User Guide. If you have questions, contact support@silvertreebrands.com."

> "Thanks for watching!"

**[SCREEN: Fade out]**

## Post-Production Notes

### Visual Overlays

Add text overlays for:
- Tier indicators: "Basic Feature" vs "Premium Feature"
- Key metrics: Label KPIs clearly
- Navigation breadcrumbs

### Callouts

Use arrows or highlights for:
- Period selector
- Brand selector (if applicable)
- Export button
- Chart interaction points

### Premium vs Basic Split

Consider creating two versions:
1. **Basic tier version**: 5 minutes, excludes Premium features
2. **Premium tier version**: Full 8 minutes

Or use chapter markers so viewers can skip to relevant sections.

### Demo Data

Use realistic numbers:
- Sales: R 50,000 - R 500,000
- Units: 500 - 5,000
- Growth rates: -10% to +30%
- Make data trends believable (seasonal peaks, etc.)

---

## Recording Tips

1. **Use Premium account** for full feature access
2. **Populate test data** beforehand for realistic charts
3. **Practice navigation** to avoid hesitation
4. **Zoom browser** to 90% if needed to fit more on screen
5. **Highlight mouse cursor** for visibility
