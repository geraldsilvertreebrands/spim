# Pricing Panel Quick Start Guide

**Track competitor prices in 5 minutes**

---

## 1. Login

- Go to your Silvertree platform URL
- Enter your pricing analyst credentials
- You'll be redirected to `/pricing`

---

## 2. View Dashboard

The dashboard shows:

- **Products Tracked**: Count with competitor data
- **Average Price Position**: Your market position
- **Recent Price Changes**: This week's changes
- **Active Alerts**: Triggered alerts count

Plus charts:
- Price position distribution
- Recent competitor price changes

---

## 3. Compare Competitor Prices

**Competitor Prices** page:

1. Click **Competitor Prices** in navigation
2. See your price vs all competitors side-by-side
3. Color coding:
   - **Green**: You're cheapest
   - **Yellow**: Mid-range
   - **Red**: Most expensive

**Filter**: Category, Price Position, Competitor

---

## 4. View Price History

**Price History** page:

1. Click **Price History** in navigation
2. Select a product from dropdown
3. View price trend chart over time
4. Your price = solid line
5. Competitors = dashed lines

**Zoom**: 7/30/90 days or custom range

---

## 5. Analyze Margins

**Margin Analysis** page:

1. Click **Margin Analysis** in navigation
2. See cost, price, margin % per product
3. View competitor avg price
4. Calculate margin if you matched competitors

Color coding:
- **Green**: > 30% margin
- **Yellow**: 15-30% margin
- **Red**: < 15% margin

---

## 6. Set Up Price Alerts

**Price Alerts** page:

1. Click **Price Alerts** → **New Alert**
2. Configure:
   - Product (or all products)
   - Competitor (or any)
   - Alert type
   - Threshold
3. Activate and save

**Alert types**:
- Competitor beats our price
- Price below threshold
- Price change > X%
- Out of stock

**Notifications**: Email + in-app bell icon

---

## 7. Import Competitor Prices

**Via CSV**:

Required columns:
- `product_sku`
- `competitor_name`
- `competitor_price`
- `scraped_at` (date/time)

Optional:
- `competitor_url`
- `competitor_sku`
- `in_stock` (true/false)

**Upload**: Settings → Import Prices → Upload CSV

---

## 8. Export Data

From any page:

1. Click **Export** button
2. Choose **CSV** or **Excel**
3. File downloads immediately

---

## Common Tasks

| Task | Steps |
|------|-------|
| **Find most expensive products** | Competitor Prices → Filter: Most Expensive |
| **Track price changes** | Price History → Select product |
| **Set up alert** | Price Alerts → New Alert |
| **Check margin** | Margin Analysis → Sort by Margin % |
| **Import data** | Settings → Import Prices |

---

## Price Comparison Matrix

For spreadsheet-style view:

1. Click **Price Comparison Matrix**
2. See all products × all competitors
3. Green = cheapest, Red = most expensive
4. Filter to "Show Only Where We're Most Expensive"

---

## Tips

✅ **Import weekly**: Keep data fresh
✅ **Set alerts for top products**: Focus on key items
✅ **Check margin before matching**: Ensure profitability
✅ **Use matrix view**: Quick repricing decisions
✅ **Export for meetings**: Share with team

---

## Data Freshness

Competitor prices are updated:
- **Manual CSV**: When you upload
- **Automated**: Check with admin (typically weekly)

Last update shown on each page.
