## Issue Priority Levels

When contacting support, indicate priority:

### P1 - Critical (1 hour response)

- System completely down
- Cannot log in (all users)
- Data loss or corruption
- Security breach

**Subject line**: `[P1 CRITICAL] Brief description`

### P2 - High (4 hour response)

- Major feature broken
- Cannot access panel
- BigQuery connection lost
- Magento sync completely failing

**Subject line**: `[P2 HIGH] Brief description`

### P3 - Medium (1 business day response)

- Minor feature issue
- Inconsistent data
- Performance degradation
- Individual user access problem

**Subject line**: `[P3 MEDIUM] Brief description`

### P4 - Low (2 business days response)

- Feature request
- Enhancement suggestion
- General question
- Documentation feedback

**Subject line**: `[P4 LOW] Brief description`

## Escalation Process

If your issue isn't resolved:

1. **Initial response**: Within stated timeframe for priority
2. **Follow-up**: If no response in 2x the timeframe, reply to your email with "ESCALATE"
3. **Management escalation**: cc: escalations@silvertreebrands.com (use sparingly for genuinely urgent unresolved issues)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I call for support?**
A: Currently, we only support email. Phone support may be added in the future.

**Q: Do you offer 24/7 support?**
A: Only for P1 Critical issues. P2-P4 are handled during business hours.

**Q: How do I report a security vulnerability?**
A: Email support@silvertreebrands.com with subject `[SECURITY]`. We'll respond within 2 hours.

**Q: Can I get a dedicated support representative?**
A: Enterprise clients can request dedicated support. Contact sales@silvertreebrands.com.

**Q: How do I check the status of my support ticket?**
A: Reply to your original email. We'll update you on progress.
