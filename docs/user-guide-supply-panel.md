## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to your Silvertree platform URL
2. Enter your supplier email and password
3. You'll be automatically redirected to the Supply Insights Portal at `/supply`

### Your Account Type

There are two supplier subscription levels:

- **Basic Tier**: Access to core insights (Dashboard, Products, Trends, Benchmarks)
- **Premium Tier**: Full access to all features including forecasting, cohort analysis, RFM, retention, and advanced analytics

You can see your current tier in the top navigation bar.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your home base for insights.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

At the top, you'll see KPIs showing:

- **Total Sales**: Revenue for the selected period
- **Units Sold**: Total units sold
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Average basket size
- **Unique Customers**: Number of unique buyers
- **Gross Margin**: Profit margin percentage (if cost data available)
- **Stock Coverage**: Days of inventory remaining

Each KPI shows:
- Current value
- Percentage change vs previous period
- Trend arrow (up/down)

### Period Selector

Change the date range using the period dropdown:

- Last 7 days
- Last 30 days
- Last 90 days
- Year to Date (YTD)
- Last Year
- Custom range

### Brand Selector

If you manage multiple brands, use the brand dropdown to filter all data to a specific brand.

### Charts

The dashboard includes:

- **Sales Trend**: Line chart showing daily/weekly/monthly sales
- **Top Products**: Bar chart of your best-selling products
- **Category Breakdown**: Pie chart of sales by product category

## Products Page

View sales performance for all your products.

### Product Table Columns

- **Product Name**: SKU and product name
- **Units Sold**: Total units sold in period
- **Revenue**: Total sales revenue
- **Average Price**: Average selling price
- **Stock Level**: Current inventory count
- **Stock Coverage**: Days until stock runs out (based on current sales velocity)

### Sorting and Filtering

- Click column headers to sort
- Use the search bar to find specific products by name or SKU
- Filter by:
  - Category
  - Stock status (in stock, low stock, out of stock)
  - Price range

### Product Deep Dive (Premium Only)

Click on a product to see:

- Detailed sales chart over time
- Customer demographics
- Purchase patterns (day of week, time of day)
- Bundling opportunities (frequently bought with)
- Reviews and ratings

## Premium Features

### Forecasting (Premium Only)

Predict future demand to optimize inventory.

**Features:**

- 30/60/90-day sales forecast
- Confidence intervals (best case, expected, worst case)
- Recommended reorder quantities
- Stockout risk alerts

**How to Use:**

1. Go to **Forecasting**
2. Select a product or category
3. View forecast chart
4. Export recommended PO quantities

### Cohorts (Premium Only)

Track customer acquisition and retention cohorts.

**What are Cohorts?**

Cohorts group customers by their first purchase month, then track their repeat purchase behavior over time.

**Example:**

- Jan 2024 cohort: Customers who first bought in Jan 2024
- Track: How many came back in Feb, Mar, Apr, etc.

**Insights:**

- Which months acquire the best customers?
- What's your typical retention curve?
- When do customers churn?

### RFM Analysis (Premium Only)

Segment customers by:

- **Recency**: When did they last buy?
- **Frequency**: How often do they buy?
- **Monetary**: How much do they spend?

**Segments:**

- **Champions**: Recent, frequent, high spenders (R5 F5 M5)
- **Loyal**: Frequent buyers, may not be recent
- **At Risk**: Used to buy frequently, now lapsed
- **Lost**: Haven't bought in a long time

**Actions:**

- Target "Champions" with new products
- Re-engage "At Risk" with promotions
- Win back "Lost" customers

### Retention (Premium Only)

Track how many customers return.

**Metrics:**

- **Day 30 Retention**: % who buy again within 30 days
- **Day 60 Retention**: % who buy again within 60 days
- **Day 90 Retention**: % who buy again within 90 days

**Retention Curve:**

Chart showing retention rate over time by cohort.

### Supply Chain (Premium Only)

Monitor inventory and supply chain health.

**Metrics:**

- Inventory turnover rate
- Days inventory outstanding (DIO)
- Stock coverage by product
- Stockout frequency

### Purchase Orders (Premium Only)

Track PO performance (if integrated).

**Metrics:**

- PO on-time delivery rate
- PO fulfillment accuracy
- Average lead time

### Customer Engagement (Premium Only)

Understand customer behavior.

**Metrics:**

- Email open rates
- Site visit frequency
- Cart abandonment rate
- Wishlist adds

### Market Share (Premium Only)

See your position in the market.

**Metrics:**

- Your % of total category sales
- Competitor comparison
- Trend over time

### Marketing (Premium Only)

Measure marketing effectiveness.

**Metrics:**

- Campaign performance
- ROI by channel
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV)

## Exporting Data

All tables and charts can be exported.

### Export Formats

- **CSV**: For Excel or Google Sheets
- **Excel**: Formatted .xlsx file

### How to Export

1. Navigate to any page with data (Products, Trends, etc.)
2. Click the **Export** button at the top right
3. Choose format (CSV or Excel)
4. File downloads immediately

### What's Exported

- All visible data based on current filters
- Respects your brand and date range selections
- Includes calculated fields like growth rates

## Tips and Best Practices

### Getting the Most Value

**Daily:**
- Check dashboard KPIs for overnight sales
- Review stock coverage for low-stock alerts

**Weekly:**
- Analyze trends to spot growth or decline
- Compare benchmarks to see competitive position
- Review top-selling products

**Monthly:**
- Run cohort analysis to understand customer retention
- Export data for deeper analysis in Excel
- Review forecasts and adjust PO quantities

### Using Brand Selector Effectively

If you manage multiple brands:

- Set a default brand in your user preferences
- Switch brands to compare performance
- Export brand-specific reports for each stakeholder

### Understanding Period Comparisons

When viewing trends:

- Use "vs Previous Period" to spot recent changes
- Use "vs Last Year" to account for seasonality
- Use YTD for annual planning

### Interpreting KPI Trends

- **Green arrow up**: Positive change (good for sales, bad for churn)
- **Red arrow down**: Negative change (bad for sales, good for churn)
- **Gray dash**: No significant change

### Forecasting Best Practices (Premium)

- Review forecasts weekly, especially for fast-moving products
- Combine forecast with current stock coverage
- Use confidence intervals to plan safety stock
- Adjust for known promotions or events

### RFM Segmentation (Premium)

- Export RFM segments for targeted marketing
- Focus retention efforts on "At Risk" segment
- Cross-sell to "Champions" segment
